Arcane S01 E09 - The Monster You Created
 Released on: 20-Nov-2021
Based on the world behind League of Legends, Arcane dives into the delicate balance between the rich, utopian city of Piltover Crest and the seedy, oppressed underground of Zaun Crest. Known across Runeterra as the "city of progress," many of the most brilliant minds call these cities home. But the creation of hextech, a way for any person to control magical energy, threatens that balance. The story follows the origins of two iconic League champions-and the power that will tear them apart. Like the game on which it is based, Arcane is aimed at a "16+" audience and will deal with some more adult subject matter.

Loki S01 E06 - For All Time. Always.
 Released on: 14-Jul-2021
After stealing the Tesseract during the events of Avengers: Endgame (2019), an alternate version of Loki is brought to the mysterious Time Variance Authority (TVA), a bureaucratic organization that exists outside of time and space and monitors the timeline. They give Loki a choice: face being erased from existence due to being a "time variant", or help fix the timeline and stop a greater threat. Loki ends up trapped in his own crime thriller, traveling through time.
